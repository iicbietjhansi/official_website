Spotless Aqua

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

"Let's start Analysing your Drinking Water Today!"

What is Water Purifier?

Water purifiers ensure that you get clean and safe drinking water on demand so that you stay away from water-borne diseases. In this event participants will be making a mini portable water purifier using the mentioned items. The efficiency of the purifier will be checked by the use of TDS meter.


Flow of Event

  • To Make a Water Purifier using plastic bottles, sand, gravel, pebbles, charcoal and cotton clothes. This event focuses on how to make an effective yet budget friendly water purifier just by few inexpensive things.
  • The participants will be making a team constituting 3 to 5 members and register for the event.
  • Testing the Purifier: 1-The efficiency of the purifier will be tested by the use of TDS meter. 2- The top 3 models with least TDS rating will be declared as winner.
  • Prize will be awarded to top 3 teams with most effiecient prufier and well structured explanation of the module.
  • Students from any school (9th to 12th standard) or any recognized institution or college can participate in this event.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
  • Participants will not be allowed to use their cell phones during the event.
  • All participants should maintain discipline and they must follow the given instructions.
  • Final decision for results will be with the jury. No objection regarding results will be entertained.
  • Students from any recognized institution or college can participate.
  • All participants must carry their institute identity cards at all time during event.
  • Use of flammable material is restricted.
