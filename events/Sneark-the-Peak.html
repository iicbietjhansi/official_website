Sneak The Peak

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“let’s touch the sky by blending the extract of chemicals”

What is Sneak the Peak?

The reaction of chemistry is so interesting and become more excited when you perform in the laboratory. Some of its reactions leave question marks that how this type of holding. Some of these types of reactions are dancing gummy bears, fire rainbow, sugar snakes and etc. Elephant toothpaste is part of the reaction. We organize an event for Elephant toothpaste.


Flow of Event

  • This event is for the testing of practical knowledge of chemical reagents and reaction and participants have to make elephant toothpaste of maximum with using prefect proportion of chemical reagents.
  • It is a single-participant event.
  • We organize a quiz round for testing participants’ knowledge of chemistry and chemical reactions.
  • Firstly participants’ have to find the potassium reagents among 5 or 6 other reagent by their color and smell specification. And then they perform elephant toothpaste reaction.
  • The prize will be distributed to the top 3 participants and all others who will be complete the task will also be awarded.
  • Undergraduate students from Indian colleges, with knowledge of basic chemical reactions.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Every Participant’ can take only 200ml of H2O2 as a maximum amount.
  • Every Participant’ can take only 10ml of Potassium reagents as a maximum amount.
  • Every participant’ have to perform the task within 15 min otherwise He/She will be disqualified.

Judging Criteria

  • In the first round top 15 participants’ who gave the maximum number of right answers will qualify for the second round.
  • Whose elephant’s toothpaste attains maximum height will announce as a winner.
  • In case two or more participants’ elephant toothpaste attains the same maximum height then the winning criteria will depend on the time span within the task will perform.

Water Purifier

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

"Let's start Analysing your Drinking Water Today!"

What is Water Purifier?

Water purifiers ensure that you get clean and safe drinking water on demand so that you stay away from water-borne diseases. In this event participants will be making a mini portable water purifier using the mentioned items. The efficiency of the purifier will be checked by the use of TDS meter.


Flow of Event

  • To Make a Water Purifier using plastic bottles, sand, gravel, pebbles, charcoal and cotton clothes. This event focuses on how to make an effective yet budget friendly water purifier just by few inexpensive things.
  • The participants will be making a team constituting 3 to 5 members and register for the event.
  • Testing the Purifier: 1-The efficiency of the purifier will be tested by the use of TDS meter. 2- The top 3 models with least TDS rating will be declared as winner.
  • Prize will be awarded to top 3 teams with most effiecient prufier and well structured explanation of the module.
  • Students from any school (9th to 12th standard) or any recognized institution or college can participate in this event.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
  • Participants will not be allowed to use their cell phones during the event.
  • All participants should maintain discipline and they must follow the given instructions.
  • Final decision for results will be with the jury. No objection regarding results will be entertained.
  • Students from any recognized institution or college can participate.
  • All participants must carry their institute identity cards at all time during event.
  • Use of flammable material is restricted.
