Today's Vinci

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“Save your eggs from the fall!”

What is Exhibition?

Exhibition is an event for participants to showcase their innovation and intelligence through their inventions. Participants will have to create a working model based on their ideas and innovation. The participants will have to bring model on the day of event.


Flow of Event

  • Students from any school (9th to 12th standard) or any recognized institution or college can participate in this event.
  • >Before the main event a briefing will be provided to the students about the event, so the participants get know well about event.
  • Participants need to register through the registration form which will be released. They will also need to submit their model idea in brief.
  • The teams will be shortlisted on the basis of projects description submitted. The shortlisted teams during this phase will be invited to present their model during the exhibition.
  • The shortlisted teams will have to display their models and explain their projects using charts and other documents. The space for the exhibition will be provided in the incubation center itself.
  • Models will be evaluated by jury members which will be from academia and industry. Marking Scheme: - Creativity and Imagination – 30% - Originality and Scientific Innovation – 20% - Technical Skills and Workmanship – 10% - Utility to Society and Scalability – 20% - Economic Viability (Low Cost) – 10% - Presentation – 10%
  • Based on jury and evaluations, results will be declared. Assured certificates for all participants. Lucrative prizes for winners.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Each team should contain minimum 3 to maximum 5 members.
  • Teams will have to keep the deadlines in mind. Extension beyond deadline will lead to disqualification..
  • All participants should maintain discipline and follow the instructions by the organizing team.
  • Final decision for results will be with the jury. No objection regarding results will be entertained.
  • All participants must carry their institute identity cards at all time during event.
  • All teams from school should be accompanied by teacher assigned by the school.
