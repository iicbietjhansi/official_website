Egg Drop

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“Save your eggs from the fall!”

What is Egg Drop?

Save the egg from breaking as it drops from a height! Participants are required to put their skills to test as they are required to protect the egg from any damage due to falling. The team that makes the best use of materials and whose egg survives the highest drop shall be named as the winner.


Flow of Event

  • Students from any school (9th to 12th standard) or any recognized institution or college can participate in this event.
  • >Before the main event a briefing will be provided to the students about the event, so the participants get know well about event.
  • Participants need to make a team constituting of 3 to 5 members and register for the event.
  • PAfter the materials are provided, a half an hour time slot shall be allotted to all teams to prepare their egg lander package. No extra time will be given.
  • The testing of lander will be done by dropping the package from a standard height. Top 3 teams that land with the egg unbroken shall be declared as the winner.
  • Prize will be awarded to top 3 teams with most efficient and well structured explanation of the module.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Use of any external materials except the ones provided is not allowed.
  • Use of cell phones is not allowed during the event.
  • All participants are required to maintain discipline and stick to the given instructions.
  • Final decision for results will be with the jury. No objection regarding results will be entertained.
  • All participants must carry their institute identity cards at all time during event.
