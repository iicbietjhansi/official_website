What is Logo Making?

Logo should communicate both who and what you are .It should speak to your audience about your goals and ideas. Every successfully start up and company’s have there respective logo which justify their business very well .We are here to challenge our designing skills and creates a alternative of such big brand logo .Example Google, meta, amazon etc.


Flow of Event

  • Students from any recognized institution or college can participate in this event.
  • >Before the main event a briefing will be provided to the students about the event, so the participants get know well about event.
  • The working time can be flexible, depending upon the complexity of the logo designing.
  • After the completion of working time, the participants have to submit the logo on google form which will be provided to you.
  • The most accurate alternative logo of respective brands by the participants will be selected.
  • The participants whose logo will be creative and justifies the business of that company will be considered winner. The winning participants will be awarded certificates, medals and sponsor rewards.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • The design of the logo must be an original work of creation. Free from plagiarism or copyright violation of any kind.
  • It is individual event no group participation is allowed.
  • Participants are required to make new logos for existing companies as per their choice from given set of logos.
  • Participants should upload the logo in any one of the PDF\JPEG\PNG format.
  • The Logo should be in high resolution with minimum 300 PPI and should look clear.
  • The logo should be designed on any digital platform only.
  • With the new logo that participants have created they have to upload the previous logo of the company which you have selected.
