Startup Conclave

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“Don’t worry about failure, you only have to be right once.”

What is Start-up Conclave?

Startup Conclave will be a vibrant platform where startups will get the opportunity to meet entrepreneurs from across the state. This will be the best chance for all the new entrepreneurs to make good contacts and expand their network. They can discuss their ideas, share their thoughts and take advice and suggestions from a diverse entrepreneurial crowd. This will also pave the way for investment discussions and funding initiatives. Apart from all this, the fest authorities have organized sessions on startups, demonstrations, workshops and other interesting activities. The event will also be graced by several investors and CEOs of several successful ventures.


Flow of Event

  • Startups need to register through the registration form that will be released. The form will include details on how to apply and the required information for submission.
  • Specific amount of space will be alloted to the Startup for showcase of their product. Teams have to arrive 4 hrs prior to event for setting up stalls.
  • A specific amount of space will be allotted to each startup for showcasing their product. To ensure a smooth setup, teams are required to arrive four hours prior to the event to set up their stalls.
  • Judges may ask about the startup or product's business model, market and competition, and financials. They may also inquire about the team, vision, and growth plans.
  • Results for the competition will be declared in a timely manner. Winners will be announced during the event or soon after.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Startups that register are expected to be present on the day of the event. Failure to comply will result in being blacklisted from participating in future events organized by the institution.
  • The institution is not responsible for providing accommodation or meals for the participants. It is the responsibility of the participants to make their own arrangements in this regard.
  • No reimbursement or travel allowance will be entertained. The participants are expected to bear the cost of their own travel and accommodation.
  • Any unruly behavior during the event will be taken seriously and appropriate action will be initiated by the institution in accordance with the law.
  • The participants will be responsible for the safekeeping of their products. The institution will not be liable for any loss or damage to the products of the participants during the event.
  • The institution will provide space for display, a table, and electricity supply for presentations. No other expectations should be made from the institution regarding the setup or facilities provided.
  • For any further assistance, participants may contact the listed event coordinators. They will be happy to assist with any queries or concerns related to the event.
