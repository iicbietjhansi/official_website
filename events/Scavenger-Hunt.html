Scavanger Hunt

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

"Hands-on experience is the best way to learn about all the interdisciplinary aspects of robotics."

What is Scavanger Hunt?

Scavenger hunt is a finding game. Participants will be provided a list of items which they have to search in the campus. The items listed may be daily use objects or in some cases participants may need to modify avaliable items to get the desired product. As the team keeps finding one or the other items on the list they will have to bring to the observation deck which will be established at some specified location. This event will be no less than a treasure hunt so be ready to feel and witness the thrill !


Flow of Event

  • All the participants will have to make teams of 3-4 members.
  • All the teams will be briefed about the rules and regulations of the event.
  • As the teams will collect the listed items , they should report back to their designated observation deck and get their item checked in the list. Items which are not presented at the observation deck within the time limit will not be entertained.
  • Around. 2-3 Hours.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • A team must contain 3-4 members.
  • Participants will have to find the objects within the time limit .
  • Any unruly behaviour will not be tolerated.
  • The winner will be decided based on points alloted to the team.
  • The decision made by observation deck will be final.
