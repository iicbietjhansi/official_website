BUILD-0-PUMP

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“let’s touch the sky by blending the extract of chemicals”

What is Build-o-Pump?

The water pump can be defined as a pump that uses the principles like mechanical as well as hydraulic throughout a piping system to make a sufficient force for its future use. In this event the participants will be making a water pump along with the impeller and its efficiency will be checked by its capability to empty the prescribed amount of water in the minimum possible time.


Flow of Event

  • The participants will be given complete details about the event and its rules, score counting procedure, negatives, and elimination. If required they will also be trained about impeller and water pump-making procedures. Reference video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=46W0uM5tDjc
  • The participants will be making a team constituting 3 to 5 members and register for the event.
  • Testing the Pump: 1- The efficiency of the pump will be tested by the time taken by it to empty 20 litres of water.
    2- In case of tie the weight to power ratio will be the final judging criteria.
  • Prize will be distributed to top 3 teams with best prototype taking least amount of time to empty the vessel and least weight to power ratio.
  • Students from any school (9th to 12th standard) or any recognized institution or college can participate in this event.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Impeller should be handcrafted and in case of any malpractice team will be disqualified.
  • Only DC-powered motor is allowed.
  • The motor should be of a maximum of 1000rpm.
  • Team must comprise 3 to 5 members.
