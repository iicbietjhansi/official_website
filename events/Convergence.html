What is After Movie?

In this event the participants have to shoot videos and click photos throughout the events in Innovanza 23 and make an aftermovie video using those videos and images.


Flow of Event

  • The Participants will be briefed about the event and rules.
  • The Participants will form a team of minimum 2 and maximum 3 and register for the event.
  • The participants will visit in the events and record videos and click photos throughout the events.
  • Finally, participants will have to compile and edit those clips and make an after movie.
  • Prizes will be awarded to top 3 winners .

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Undergraduate college students from any Indian college, with knowledge in video making can participate.
  • A team must contain minimum 2 and maximum 3 members.
  • No editing software with watermark will be allowed.
  • Discipline should be maintained throughout the event.
  • Participants will have to use BIICF logo in the video as watermark.
