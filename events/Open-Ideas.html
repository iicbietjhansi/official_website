Open Mic for Ideas

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“It’s not about ideas. It’s about making ideas happen."

What is Open Mic for Ideas?

Open mic for ideas is a platform where innovators and budding entrepreneurs who have not yet stepped into the world of startup but have an innovative solution for a prevailing problem can come and present their idea using whatever means they feel fit. This event will provide them a stage to not only validate their idea but also to have public feedback on the solutions they propose. They can also avail mentorship from our mentors and experts of our event. Top 10 ideas which will be selected on the basis of reviews from the experts will be receiving prizes to not only boost their morale but also help them convert their ideas into real products and commercialize them.


Flow of Event

  • Participants have the option to register for the event through the website in advance or on spot on the day of the event. This allows for flexibility and convenience for all participants.
  • The idea for the presentation should be presented in a time frame of 5-10 minutes, allowing for a concise and effective delivery of the concept.
  • Judges may ask about the startup or product's business model, market and competition, and financials. They may also inquire about the team, vision, and growth plans.
  • Results for the competition will be declared in a timely manner. Winners will be announced during the event or soon after.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Presentations can be made in either Hindi or English language, giving participants the freedom to choose the language they are most comfortable with.
  • There are no restrictions on the mode of participation, allowing participants to participate in the way that best suits their strengths and abilities.
  • Participation can be done either as an individual or as part of a team, giving participants the flexibility to choose the format that they prefer.
  • Participants will be allotted a time slot of 5 to 10 minutes for their presentation, allowing them to effectively convey their ideas within a specific time frame.
  • Any unruly behavior during the event will be taken seriously and action will be initiated by the institution by law.
